# Typical Sizes

Use these as default modeling assumptions unless the drawings or floor plans show otherwise.

## Typical Elements

- slab: 8 in typical
- columns: 12 x 24 in typical
- walls: 10 in typical

## Typical Placement

- column locations will be shown in imported CAD

## Typical Behavior

- pinned connections are typical
- pinned columns are typical
- shear walls resist all lateral load
